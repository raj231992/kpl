Gameplay Rules


  1. Each match will be of 8 overs out of which 1 over will be exclusively for Female Player

  2. Win fetches 2 points

  3. No balls/wides/any other illegal deliveries to be penalised with 2 runs

  4. A Free hit will be given for a no-ball

  5. There is super over in 1-4 overs, which is taken by batting team and in this over each run scored by batsman will be counted as double. Runs for wide and no-ball will remain unchanged

  6. Maximum 2 bowlers can bowl 2 overs each

  7. If any batsman scores 30 runs in an inning then he has to retire and can come again for batting after all the batsman of his team

  8. Batsman can not be retire in super over if he reaches 30 runs

  9. Opponent team(if present in full strength) will be declared winners if the other team does not assemble in full(7 male + 1 female players) strength within 30 minutes of the given time

  10. In case of a tie, super over will be played. In case of another tie, scores of the female players will be considered

  11. Top 4 teams qualify for play-off stage; ties will be resolved using NRR (net run rate)



Rules for Female Players


  1. Only one over (full over) to be bowled and faced; (5th Over)

  2. Bowling will be from half length of the pitch and using the `throwâ€™ action

  3. Only 4 Male fielders will be on ground during this over, however boundaries will remain unchanged

  4. Extras for female players will cost 1 run only (instead of 2 runs)

  5. Male teammate runs from the other end(full pitch)

  6. In case the player gets out, team loses 2 runs and the over continues. Incase the match gets over before 5th over, the female player still need to bowl as it may lead to negative runs also

  7. Female player has to field all through the inning



Franchise and Bidding rules


  1. Manager has to register on the webiste and pay 3000 INR to buy a team

  2. Maximum 8 teams, to be decided on first come basis

  3. All bidding happens with virtual money (players do not get any money)

  4. Manager can play by as part of the team. An amount of 75% of highest bidden player in his team will be deducted from the virtual money during the auction (only for male manager)

  5. NO BIDDING FOR FEMALE PLAYERS
